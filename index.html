Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, booking process, and policies.

How do I book the best taxi service with Ride Lanka Cabs?
Booking the best taxi service with Ride Lanka Cabs is simple! Visit our website booking page, call our hotline, or send us a WhatsApp message. Simply enter your pickup and drop locations, select your preferred vehicle, and confirm. Whether you need a taxi near me or a long-distance cab service, we've got you covered instantly.
Does Ride Lanka Cabs provide airport transfer Sri Lanka?
Yes, absolutely! We specialize in premium airport transfers in Sri Lanka, providing reliable, punctual pickups and drops to and from Bandaranaike International Airport (CMB) 24/7. We also provide complementary flight tracking to ensure our driver is waiting for you right on time.
Are your drivers for the taxi service near me verified?
Your safety is our top priority. All chauffeurs driving for our taxi service near me undergo rigid background checks, possess professional driving licenses, and have years of accident-free experience. They are also courteous and highly familiar with routes island-wide.
What makes Ride Lanka Cabs the best cab service in Sri Lanka?
We are rated as the best cab service due to our unmatched combination of absolute transparency, fixed pricing with zero hidden costs, 24/7 client support, a highly diverse luxury fleet, and professionally attired expert local drivers.
What is the pricing for your taxi service in Sri Lanka?
We offer highly competitive and fully transparent fixed pricing based on the vehicle class selected and the destination distance. Once a rate is quoted to you upon booking confirmation, it remains final—with no hidden midnight charges or surge fees.
How far in advance can I book an airport transfer?
You can book your airport transfer weeks or months in advance to secure your preferred vehicle type. For emergency or last-minute rides, our 24/7 rapid dispatch system is also capable of arranging a vehicle within minutes.
What vehicles are available in your cab service fleet?
Our extensive fleet ranges from fuel-efficient mini cars (Wagon R, Alto) and comfortable family sedans (Prius, Axio) to premium dual A/C vans (KDH Flat/High roof) and large-capacity luxury coaches/buses suitable for wedding or corporate hire.
Do you offer taxi service near me 24/7?
Yes! Our dispatch network runs seamlessly 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year. No matter if it's a late-night flight arrival or an early morning emergency travel plan, Ride Lanka Cabs is always active.
Can I cancel my cab service booking?
Yes, cancellations can be done smoothly. We kindly request you to notify us at least a few hours prior to the scheduled pickup time so we can reroute our vehicles efficiently without any hassle.
Do you offer tour packages with your taxi service?
Yes, we offer custom multi-day tour packages all across Sri Lanka (covering major attractions like Ella, Sigiriya, Nuwara Eliya, Galle, Trincomalee, and Arugam Bay). Our experienced route-expert drivers double as great tour guides for your holiday!
